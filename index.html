Auton's Diner


Auton's Diner is a VR game requiring only a VR compatible browser such as Firefox on a mobile device to be playable in VR mode. Select the VR button to begin, look around and tap the burgers to start flipping! Auton's Answers is a series dedicated to special needs awareness.
